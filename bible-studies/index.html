Sunday Adult Bible Study

Sunday Mornings 10/24 - 12/12

9:50am, Zoom available

There is a new Sunday School series starting this Sunday on the Incarnation of Jesus, facilitated by Jason Tolbert. Why did Jesus have to come in the flesh? Why was that necessary for salvation? Why did He have to be crucified? Was there no other way for our sins to be forgiven? We will delve into these questions and many more in our next Adult Sunday School series on the incarnation of Jesus Christ. This will be a six week series starting on October 24 in Klein Hall every Sunday at 9:50 am, and will run through December 12 (a two week break is included for Thanksgiving and the voter's meeting).
