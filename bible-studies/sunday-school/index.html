Sunday School

Living Savior Lutheran Church has a variety of educational resources for your family. From Sunday School, to weekly Adult Bible Studies, Youth activities and Vacation Bible School in the Summer, we strive to help you deepen your knowledge of Christ and Scripture.

Children

Children’s Sunday School begins at 9:45am each Sunday morning divided into two age groups.

  • Pre-K through 2nd grade
  • 3rd grade through fifth grade

If you have any questions, please contact Sarah Loesch at srw002@comcast.net

Schedule

We start on the Sunday after Labor Day (Rally Day) and finish the first Sunday in June.

Nursery

LSLC has a nursery provided for small children (ages 4 and younger) during the worship services and for Sunday School. Susanna Honeycutt is the attendant who watches the children, provides a short, age appropriate lesson during the services, and brings older children (ages 3-4) to the PreK-K Sunday School class during Sunday School, if the parent chooses. Please register your child with Susanna in the Nursery, located in the educational wing.

Confirmation

Confirmation instruction is a two year program for our young people, where we study the basic building blocks of our Christian faith as it is found in Martin Luther’s Small Catechism. This class is designed for 6th and 7th graders, and meets in the Confirmation Room on Sunday mornings during the Bible Study hour.

Adults

We have one Adult Sunday School class that meets in Klein Hall on Sundays. We are blessed with a variety of excellent lay teachers who provide informative and thought provoking lessons on Scripture and Church History, as well as a great group of students who share thoughts and testimonies of the impact of God’s word in their lives.
