description: In truth, every Ministry (and every congregant!) at Living Savior is a means to Evangelize.  We reach out to the community to share the Gospel by both word and deed.  Sometimes in partnership with other Ministries such as Fellowship, Social, and Music, we host and participate in community outreach efforts such as concerts, Open Houses, picnics, Christmas Tree sales, and other service events to which we invite friends, neighbors, and co-workers, while advertising them as well to include our local community.  We are also heavily involved with our Vicar in the expanding George Mason University Campus Ministry.  Make a difference, and become an active part of fulfilling our Great Commission - join us in helping spread the Good News!
