title: Young Family
leads: Ben and Victoria Pierce
description: Young family ministry is a fellowship and faith enriching opportunity for the family and friends of Living Savior. We meet once a month for an activity to have some fun, build relationships, and experience God’s word in action.  Our primary focus is families with children from preschool age through fifth grade, but we welcome all families!  We seek to create an environment where the children and families of Living Savior know and feel their importance to the Church body.
