description: The Music Ministry offers many opportunities for personal involvement in the Worship Service. The Chancel Choir leads the singing of hymns and liturgy and presents scripture-based anthems each Sunday. Solos and musical ensembles also provide opportunities for worshipers to participate. Sunday school children present a Christmas Program. Worship is our response to what God has done for us through Jesus. "Music has the power to take us into the realm of the Spirit."
