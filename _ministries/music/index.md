title: Music
leads: Lois Finnern - Interim Minister of Worship/Organist Dr. Wallace Horton
description: The Music Ministry offers many opportunities for personal involvement in the Worship Service.  The Chancel Choir leads the singing of hymns and liturgy, presents a scripture-based anthem each Sunday, and performs an annual Christmas Cantata.  Instrumentalists and vocalists in the Contemporary Praise Band lead the Worship Service with modern songs.  Solos and musical ensembles provide opportunities for worshipers to participate.  Sunday school children perform a Christmas Program.  Music has the power to light up scripture, give life to the words, impress it on the heart, and express the joy it brings!
