Andrew Lissy Pastor

Pastor Andy was raised in northern New Jersey. He graduated from Concordia College, Bronxville New York, and Concordia Seminary St. Louis. He also has a Master of Arts in counseling from Rider University, Lawrenceville, New Jersey. Pastor's first call was as Assistant Pastor, Hope Lutheran Church and School in Levittown, Pennsylvania. He later was called as Senior Pastor of that congregation. In 2002, Pastor Andy accepted the call to be Senior Pastor First English Lutheran Church in New Orleans, Louisiana. Pastor served there through Hurricane Katrina. He was installed at Living Savior January of 2007.

Pastor Andy's wife, Natalie, is an assistant principal in Fairfax County Public School. They have four grown children.

Scot Missling Vicar

Scot was born and raised in Minnesota, growing up in Plymouth, a western suburb of the Minneapolis-St. Paul area. Scot graduated in 2014 from Augustina University in Sioux Falls, SD with a degree in Government and International Affairs. He pursued a career in government and spent 3.5 years working for the Minnesota Legislature as a Staff Assistant to several members of the Minnesota Senate, and eventually as a Staff Assistant to the Senate Majority Leader.

Scot and Veronica met in 2017 when we both worked at the Minnesota Legislature and were married in January 2021. They are expecting their first child in February 2022.
