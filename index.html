Living Savior Lutheran Church is a Christian community of the Lutheran Church - Missouri Synod.

We believe

That God’s love is shown in the life, death, and resurrection of his Son, Jesus Christ.
That God offers a free gift of eternal life to all to believe in Jesus Christ.
That the gift is given to all who receive it through the Holy Spirit.
That we gather for worship, study, fellowship and service as a response to God’s love for us.

At A Glance

We are glad you are with us today! Visitors are welcome and asked to sign the Guest Register and Welcome Book in the Narthex through the main entrance.

Worship Services: Every Sunday Morning at 8:30 AM and 11:00 AM

Sunday School: Children, youth and adult Bible classes every Sunday at 9:45 AM

Located At: 5500 Ox Road, Fairfax Station, VA 22039 (Directions)
