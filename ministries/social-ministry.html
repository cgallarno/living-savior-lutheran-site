Social Ministry at LSLC

Social Ministry at LSLC

Join Living Savior Lutheran Church in its mission to be the hands and feet of Christ as we work to serve our neighbors in need. Email Megan Browning at Megs123450@gmail.com to get involved in the following opportunities.

Moving Kits
Provide families moving into new homes with food, kitchen, and laundry supplies.

Lamb’s Center
Gifts to the Lamb Center provide lifesaving resources such as food, mental health services and more.

Concordia Food Ministry
Ongoing food drive located outside of Klein Hall to provide non-perishable pantry items to those in need. Watch the food & clothing ministry video.

Global Refugee
Providing resources, guidance, and community to help forge a way forward for New Americans.

Backpack Drive
Donate school supplies to support the Domestic Violence Shelter Artemis House.

Crop Hunger Walk
Support those with food insecurity by walking together to raise funds and awareness.

Thanksgiving Baskets
Provide a holiday meal to families at the Domestic Violence Artemis House.

Hypothermia Shelters
Support to those experiencing homelessness during the winter months by providing food, shelter, and comfort during inclement weather.

Angel Tree
Join LSLC in its holiday assistance program that connects a sponsor with a deserving child “Angel” in need.

Operation Christmas Child
Join the Samaritan’s purse in its mission to provide gift-filled shoeboxes to children in over 100 countries to share the gospel.
