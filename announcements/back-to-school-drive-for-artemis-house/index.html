Back to School drive for Artemis House

Back to School is not "back to normal" this year.
In the Year of COVID, LSLC has coordinated with the Artemis House domestic violence shelter to help provide what kids need to start up school. Fairfax County is fully remote learning until January so the list looks different from past years.
If you can donate items, please check the "What to Bring" list in this invitation. Drop it off at LSLC by August 20th.

If you cannot shop or drop off, monetary donations are most welcome. Just mail a check in to LSLC ( you can included that check in your offering envelope) or donate on-line (indicate Social Ministry).
