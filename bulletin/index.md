# This Sunday's Service


#### January 29th, 2017

We are glad you are with us and pray a blessing on your worship today! Visitors are asked to sign the **Guest Register** on the table in the Narthex (entrance) and sign the **Welcome Book** giving your address and phone number.

**Living Savior’s MISSION** is to know the love of Christ and serve God so that the Holy Spirit may work through us to bring others to Christ.

**Nursery is provided for small children ages 4 and younger during the worship services and Sunday School** from 8:15am to 12:15pm in room #2 in the Educational Wing. Susanna Honeycutt is the attendant. Please register your children in the nursery so she can get to know them.

**Choir Notes:** Chancel Choir rehearsal is held on Wednesday evenings at 7:30pm. Voices are needed for all parts and Sr High through adults are welcome and encouraged to join. The choir supports the singing of the hymns and liturgy, as well as presenting a special anthem each Sunday and at special services during the year. The choir sings at the communion service, alternating between the 8:30 and 11:00 service each week. If you enjoy singing, we need you for this important ministry. The contemporary praise band rehearsals are held on Thursday evenings at 7:30pm. The contemporary services are held on the first and third Sunday of each month at the 11:00 service, with rehearsals being held on the Thursday evenings prior to the first and third Sundays. Vocalists and instrumentalists are encouraged to join this group for our contemporary services. Contact Lois Finnern with any questions at 703-913-1728 or llfinnern@aol.com.

**Men's Prayer Breakfast:** Men of the congregation are invited to join other Living Savior men for breakfast, prayer and Bible study every 2nd and 4th Saturday from 7 - 8:30am. Questions? Contact Paul Gross.

**Men’s Christian Fellowship Dinner:** LSLC men meet the last Thursday of every month beginning at 7:00pm for dinner, fellowship and a short Bible study. The next dinner will be January 26th at 7:00pm. Questions? Contact Jim Neuburger.

**Women In Prayer** meets Saturdays at 9:30am. We are continuing with a study of women of the bible. If you are interested in being on the e-mail list, please email meganbyer7@gmail.com.

**Lifelight** is an in-depth bible study which meets on Wednesdays during the school year from 1:00-2:30pm in the Narthex and is led by Donna Dietz. We are doing a study of the Book of Luke. Please contact Terri Beam with any questions at 703-239-0782 or terribeam@gmail.com. All are welcome!

**Small Group Home Bible Study:** come for food, fellowship, and a Bible study! The next meeting will be Feb. 5th from 1-3pm at the Heetland’s. Bring a dish to share and your Bibles! Questions? Please contact Henni Froemke, 703-635-2131.

**Adult Sunday School - every Sunday between services we shall continue discussing "Jesus' Words to Seven Churches of Roman Asia" (Revelation 1-3)**, continuing with the church at Pergamum. That continues our discussion of each church addressing the scriptural background to Jesus' words and historical facts - environment, achievements and failures - about each church, and how they apply to the Church today. For more information contact Jim MacGregor, scotjim@earthlink.net.

COMMUNION POLICY: Everyone who is a baptized Christian, repents of sin, believes in the real presence of Christ's body and blood in, with, under the bread and wine of the Holy Communion is cordially invited to receive the Lord's Supper at Living Savior. To receive wine by individual cup, take a cup from the serving tray and wait for Pastor to say "The Blood of Christ", then return the cup to server that follows Pastor. To receive wine by the common cup (chalice), take the base & tilt to receive the amount you wish. If you wish to receive nonalcoholic white grape juice, there are individual cups in the center of the tray. If you wish to receive Gluten/wheat/corn and soya free communion wafers please speak to Pastor or an usher before the start of service.
