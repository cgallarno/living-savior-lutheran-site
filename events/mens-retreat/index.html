Men's Retreat 2022

                                                                                

THEME: “Citizens in Two Worlds” 

Roslyn Conference Center

Richmond, Virginia

September 16-18, 2022

The 2022 Living Savior Lutheran Church Men’s Retreat will be held at the Roslyn Conference Center (RCC) Friday September 16th through Sunday September 18th.  The theme of our retreat: “Citizens in Two Worlds”.  We are honored to have Pastor Brad Singer (former LSLC vicar) as our facilitator.

Food for thought:  As Christians, we are called to live as good citizens of two worlds: the heavenly and the earthly. Throughout American history, many Lutherans have served both worlds faithfully, greatly blessing America in military and civic service. The question remains: how does our earthly citizenship help us understand our heavenly citizenship, and how does our heavenly citizenship help us faithfully live out our earthly citizenship?

Detailed information about the RCC can be found on their website:  roslyncenter.org
