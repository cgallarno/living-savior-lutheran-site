LSLC Christmas Tree Sales

Sales information - Trees 6' to 9'

Times of Operation:

  • Saturdays - 10:00am to 5:00pm
  • Sundays - 12:00 noon to 5:00pm
  • Weds, Thurs, Fridays - 6:00pm - 8:30pm

Funds generated will be donated to various charities. For example, last year LSLC donated $6K to Lutheran School Service (Afghan Relief), Ukrainian Relief, World Relief, Concordia Seminary, and tress overseas missions.

Sign up to Help

The trees arrive on Friday, 25 November (time TBD). Sales will begin after the tress are unloaded. Volunteers are asked to take 1–2-hour blocks. Families are encouraged to sign up for the weekends. We will also be adding a weekly manager to help spread the load. This manager will be responsible for recruiting to fill their week, guiding the sellers with questions and being on call for help.
