Sales information – Trees 6’ to 9’
November 25 through December 23 (or when we run out of trees)
Times of Operation:
    Saturdays - 10am to 5pm
    Sundays - 12 noon to 5pm
    Weeknights - 6:00pm to 8:30pm

Funds generated will be donated to various charities.  For example, last year LSLC donated to Concordia Food Bank (Triangle, VA), Lutheran School Service, Concordia Seminary, and three overseas missions.

Sign up to Help
The trees are due to arrive on the morning of Friday 24 November. Once the trees are unloaded, we will immediately commence selling. If the weather cooperates, we should be able to sell most (if not all) the trees by Sunday 17 December.

Volunteer opportunities:

  1. unload trees (24 Nov; time TBD; most likely late morning)
  2. sell trees (we'll sell every day of the week; Saturday and Sunday sales are typically the best) Sign up for a time slot
  3. Team leads: we'll need 4 team leaders for the following periods: 24-30 November; 1-8 December; 9-15 December; 16-17 Sign up for a week of management

Prayerfully consider how you can help. Selling Christmas trees is a big undertaking, requiring support from the entire congregation. If you have not sold before we can teach you.

Please contact Steve Galing at Steven_Galing@msn.com if you have any questions.
