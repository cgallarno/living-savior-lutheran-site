##### October Happenings

Living Savior Preschool opened October 1st for the new school year. We’re so excited to have the children with us again! Due to COVID restrictions we aren’t able to have any in house programs or field trips at this time so things are a bit different than they’ve been in the past. Still, our year is off to a great start and the children all seem to love being back in school!

##### Chapel Lessons

Each Wednesday morning throughout the school year, the children at Living Savior gather in the Sanctuary for a Chapel lesson with either Pastor Andy or Vicar Tim. This is an important part of our annual curriculum at Living Savior Preschool as we teach the word of God through song and prayer and try to relate the message of the bible story so that it has meaning in the children’s young lives.  We look forward to another wonderful year of Chapel lessons as we continue to teach the children that Jesus touches every part of our day!
