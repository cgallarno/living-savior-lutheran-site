Newsletter Input 31 August 2020

The mission of Living Savior is to know the love of Christ and serve God so the Holy Spirit can work through us so we can bring others to Christ. While we need to take safety precautions during the pandemic, our mission continues. We strengthen our faith by attending worship (in-person or on-line), regularly studying the Word, supporting each other, and reaching out to the community.

I want to thank the 2019-2020 Board of Servant Leaders (BOSL), led by Chuck Froemke, for their dedication and tireless work. The BOSL tackled COVID-19 by developing and implementing meticulous health precautions, insuring we have weekly Services while protecting parishioners. Of particular note are the on-going efforts of the Worship team: Pastor Lissy, Vicar Brad (now Vicar Tim), Dr. Horton, Dr. Benson, Candie Jefferson, Sid Heetland, Mary Heetland, Dave Huxsoll, Lori Hults, and Cameron Gallarno. Their dedication and unwavering commitment is a shining example of living one’s faith.

Volunteer opportunities abound. Social Ministry, Outreach, the Pre-School, and Communication (i.e., live streaming of our weekly worship service) are the most visible to the public, but other ministries such as Education, Property, Youth, and Stewardship need volunteers as well. I encourage everyone to actively support at least one ministry throughout the year.

Blessings,
<br>Steve Galing
